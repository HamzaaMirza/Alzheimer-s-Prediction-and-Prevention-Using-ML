# Alzheimer-s-Prediction-and-Prevention-Using-ML
A comprehensive project that involves going through numerous patient's medical data and determine what factors contribute highly in development of Alzheimer's disease in those patients and what factors best  help us diagnose the presence of Alzheimer's disease. 
The repository consists of a pptx presentation which I created and presented in college and goes through the entire methodology and the findings of this project
Secondly, it contains the jupyter notebook that contains the entire code and results of my findings and you can go through the jupyter notebook and understand my approach and my findings.
